ABOUT

YUVAAN witnessed the presence of artists of varied domains of literature and cinematography. Lead by the soul motto to scale up and connect literature elites across India , YUVAAN constantly venture to onboard best of best. Yuvaan has been graced by DTU's own alumni and national bestseller, Durjoy Datta, artists of the viral slam poetry group UnErase Poetry, the wonderful Nizami Brothers who gave the perfect closing ceremony in the form of a Sufi Night, Major General GD Bakshi, celebrated journalists such as Sunetra Chaudhary, Rohit Sardana, Sanket Upadhyay and many more. Yuvaan has set high bars and is ready to become the biggest college literary fests in India. Right from famous content creators to great authors, poets, artists all assemble to mark the greatest literature extravaganza.
